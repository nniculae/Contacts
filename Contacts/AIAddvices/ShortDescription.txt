The Management Contacts application is a software tool designed to help users efficiently organize, 
store, and manage their contact information. Users can add new contacts, 
edit existing contact details, and search for specific contacts within the application.
The app provides basic functionalities such as adding, editing, and searching for contacts, 
with the potential for further expansion to include features such as categorization,
reminders, exporting contacts, integration with email clients, and more.
With its user-friendly interface and essential contact management features,
the app aims to streamline contact organization and communication for individuals and businesses alike.
